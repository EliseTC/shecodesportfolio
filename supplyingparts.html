Supplyingparts


Objective

Supplyingparts is a small Dutch company that works in the high-tech industry. My client was looking to build a website which needed to meet a set of specific requirements. The website was mostly designed and constructed, but the client needed help with the information architecture and copy.

After speaking to the client to understand the needs of the company and its users, I proposed an alternative information architecture and a condensed version and rewritten version of the text. We met and tweaked my proposal to satisfy all needs. Afterwards I was asked to translate the text into Dutch. This part of the job is in progresss.
