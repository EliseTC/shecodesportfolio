NewDay: Developer Portal





The challenge

The NewDay developer portal faced a series of challenges that need be resolved to enable future growth and an improved user experience. Problems include:

  • Limited discoverability: the portal didn’t effectively showcase the platform's capabilities, making it difficult for decision-makers to understand its value proposition and for developers to find the resources they need.
  • Inconsistent user experience: it’s not obvious to users where they can find the products or APIs they are after.
  • Insufficient developer support: the developer portal did not offer essential support elements, such as onboarding tools, clear changelogs, robust support documentation and so forth.














NewDay developer portal right now

Analytics show that as of May 2025 the Developer Portal overhaul has reduced time spent searching for API references by 50%, moreover clients that previously could't self-serve have had no trouble finding the information they are after.
