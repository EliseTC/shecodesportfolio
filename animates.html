AniMates


Objective

Design a product that enables people to look after multiple plants when they are at home and when they have to leave them in the care of another.

  • Duration: 12 weeks
  • Toolkit: Figma, Miro
  • Team: a team of 3 for the first five weeks, then solo
  • Medium: read about the process

VIDEO

User research

Desk research shows that increasingly many young people are interested in owning and caring for plants. Rearing plants gives a sense of purpose, helps them disconnect from virtual life, enables them to destress and enhances their mental wellbeing.

Observations in my direct surroundings show that while people love their plants, they encounter two problems. On the one hand they struggle with overwatering plants or giving them water too sparingly. On the other hand, they want to be able to go on holiday, leaving others to look after their plants. Or they may be ask themselves to look after a friend's indoor garden, causing them worry to accidentally kill their shrubs.

Next steps
